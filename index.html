Timothy Bartolf is a developer, artist, writer, and outdoorsman equipt with skills and theoretical foundations essential for navigating the contemporary world and beyond.

Professional Experience

Tim has over 4 years of professional experience working for non-profit organizations in VR, AR, and other emerging technologies. He has worked on various experimental augmented reality projects, written accredited high-school cirriculum, represented organizations at national confrences, and maintained critical information technology infrastructure across 100 devices. Tim developed and taught a nationally adopted course framework and accompanying application, A Way Out Of No Way, which was recognized at the Anthem Awards for Winning Diversity, Equity, & Inclusion Awareness Categories-Partnership or Collaboration submission.

Formal Education

Awarded a BS in Philosophy and Minor in Creative Technology Design at University of Colorado Boulder, Tim has contined his education through participating in post-graduate research collaborations.

Art and Developement

Tim has extensive experience in the field of creative technology design including contributions to game design, and experimental AR/VR technolgy. In his free time, he mainly focuses on personal level design projects available on the steam workshop.

Writing

Having written extensively on philosophy, social theory, art, Tim has a repository of writings mostly revolve around engagement with other relevant and influential thinkers from the past and present.

Outdoor Experience

Tim is currently a candidate for Marin County Search and Rescue, participating in monthly informational and training events. He has experienced backpacker, downhill mountian biker, climber, and caver.
